# Product notes (open-source starter)

## Who this is for

- Overseas B2B buyers evaluating a Chinese factory (specs, certs, capacity).
- Chinese factory / export teams who need a credible English inquiry site.

## Purpose

A data-driven Next.js template: structure from `blueprint.json`, copy/products from `content-pack.json`, look from `theme.json`. Built for RFQ conversion, not e-commerce checkout.

## Voice

Precision, restrained, parameter-first. Specs over slogans. No marketing fluff ("premium quality").

## Out of scope

Cart/payment, multi-tenant CMS, pixel-perfect clones of competitors.
